How to Train Your Dog to Lie Down

Steps:

  1. Start in a quiet and distraction-free environment.
  2. Hold a treat close to your dog's nose and slowly lower it to the ground.
  3. As your dog follows the treat, guide them into a lying-down position.
  4. Once they are down, reward them with the treat and praise.
  5. Repeat the process, gradually phasing out the treat.

How to Train Your Dog to Give a Handshake

Steps:

  1. Use a treat to get your dog's attention.
  2. Hold the treat in your hand, close to your dog's paw.
  3. Encourage your dog to lift their paw to touch your hand.
  4. When they do, reward them with the treat and praise.
  5. Practice the handshake command regularly.


How to Train Your Dog to Sit

Steps:

  1. Start in a quiet and distraction-free environment.
  2. Show your dog a treat, and hold it close to their nose.
  3. Slowly move the treat upward and over their head, causing them to sit.
  4. Once they are in a sitting position, reward them with the treat and praise.
  5. Repeat the process, gradually phasing out the treat.
